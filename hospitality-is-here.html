#HospitalityIsHere

At Your Service Staffing launched the “Hospitality is Here” fundraising campaign in March 2021 to celebrate the resilience of our industry while raising money for those in need as we marked the return of Hospitality by making a donation for every #HospitalityIsHere selfie posted on social media until June 2021!

We raised over $20,000.

So much has changed since the start of our #HospitalityIsHere Campaign. Through posting the hashtag, sharing the message, and recording your videos, you have helped raise over $20,000 for the nonprofits we are donating to: Live Events Coalition of New York and New Jersey supporting the SEARCH Foundation, the Greg Hill Foundation supporting the Restaurant Strong Fund and The SHFM Foundation. Special thanks to Snapchef for donating $5,000, and to Sodexo for donating $10,000 to the SHFM Foundation through our campaign. We are now at a time where the very same displaced hospitality workers we have been raising money for are able to get back to work, and we are so excited to work with them, and we know you are too!
