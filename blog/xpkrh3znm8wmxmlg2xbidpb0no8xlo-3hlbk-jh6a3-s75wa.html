The Hospitality Buzz is BACK!

The Hospitality Buzz is BACK and hosted by Jeanine Cosgrove-Albert, Vice President of At Your Service Staffing! This candid conversation is filled with hot takes from foodservice & hospitality experts, thought leaders & influencers. Topics range from real-time responses to COVID19 pandemic setbacks, to take this time to test new technology like, mobile ordering and apps for use when employees return to work. The panelists share best practices and the importance of leaning into networks like SHFM for support & guidance. We are proud to be part of this industry and we will get through this together.

Meet The Buzz Line-Up of Leading Hospitality and Foodservice Experts & Influencers:

  • Mark Freeman, Global Strategic Manager for Foodservice, Ford Motor Company

  • Damian Monticello Sr. Manager, Corporate Hospitality Services, GuideWell

  • Robert Gordon, VP of Workplace Services, American Express

  • Sharon Eliatamby, Sr. Program Manager, Food & Conference Services, World Bank Group

  • Jeanine Cosgrove-Albert, Vice President, At Your Service Staffing

A special thanks to the entire team for participating in this important conversation. Each person contributed insightful perspectives and the dialog was full of hope for the next steps forward.
