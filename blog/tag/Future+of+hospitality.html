AYS Spotlight Series: Sunita Devi, Director, Kimmel Center, New York University

Jeanine Cosgrove-Albert, Vice President, At Your Service Staffing, had the pleasure to sit down with higher education event leader & PR expert, Sunita Devi, Director, Kimmel Center, New York University. Sunita Devi brings years of experience and expertise to this candid conversation and graciously shared her thoughts on important COVID-19 event related issues like:

  • The commitment NYU has made to COVID-19 protocols & the impact it will have on the daily lives of students and faculty on campus.

  • The changes being made to events procedures & campus event spaces as students return this fall.

  • A glimpse into the behind-the-scenes logistics & planning for scheduled events in fall 2020.

  • The importance of connection, leveraging relationships and tapping into industry networks like, the Society of Foodservice and Hospitality Management (SHFM), to help weather the constant changes in the event industry and learn how to best use this time to be strategic in planning the future.

  • The impact that those who are still employed can make in the name of those who have lost their jobs due to the impact of COVID-19.

AYS Virtual Summer Series: Victoria Vega, SVP, Business Excellence, Unidine

At Your Service Staffing Vice President, Jeanine Cosgrove-Albert, had the pleasure to sit down with foodservice pioneer Victoria Vega, SVP, Business Excellence, Unidine. Vega brings over 30 years of innovative foodservice & culinary expertise to this conversation and shared her thoughts on leadership during the COVID-19 crisis, the impact felt by her team and adaptations Unidine, a premium healthcare foodservice provider, has made to continue to support clients during the pandemic.
